Bio

  • My research focus is on computer vision and artificial intelligence, particularly deep learning techniques, and their application to intelligent and autonomous vehicles.
  • 2015 - now: Ph.D. Program in Electrical Engineering, Electronics and Automation at Universidad Carlos III de Madrid, under supervision of Prof. José María Armingol and Dr. David Martín.
  • 2013 - 2014: Researcher in the INDIO project. Patent pending.
  • I'm helping in the preparation of the IEEE ICVES 2018 conference. Check it out!
